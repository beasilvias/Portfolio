Who is Bea Silvia

Half Engineer, half Designer. Half analytic mind, half creative mind. When it comes to define myself, it is difficult to find the right words. In this case, instead of defining my skills, I will talk about my personality.

“Skills can be learnt. Personality comes from within".

I love to listen

I love to listen, to understand and to learn. I love to meet new cultures, new languanges and new ways of thinking (specially those completely different from me).

“When you speak, you’re speaking what you know already. But when you listen, you might end up learning something new."

I trust the process

No process, no life. In both professional and personal life. It is important to know how to enjoy the journey, how to live the present. Everyone’s rushing to get to the top, all the while failing to realize that the “top” is just a mindset; it can be limitless if you let it be. Design taught me a lot on this.

"Good design comes from iterating, and the same goes for life.”

I love quotes

Yes, if you didn't notice yet, I love quotes. But the truth is that I actually love to reflect on life and human minds. Specially about Self-Discovery, and how understanding ourselves and rule our mind can empower our personal and professional life. Going out of my comfort zone helped me a lot to grow in self discovery.
